My Projects

eduAssist.me

Helping teachers around the world use their phones to perform basic classroom tasks.

eduAssist aims to provide a mobile solution to teachers with limited access to desktop computers or landline internet access. Teachers are offered an efficient solution to take class attendance quickly every morning and a gradebook suited to their own needs.
Coded as the final project of the FullStack program @LeWagon with a team of 3 other awesome coders.
